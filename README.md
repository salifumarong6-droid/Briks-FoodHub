# 🍽️ Briks-Foodhub

**The Gambia's #1 Food Business Directory**

Briks-Foodhub is an online platform that connects 
food businesses with clients across The Gambia. 
Restaurants, caterers, vendors, and food suppliers 
can list for free — and customers can discover and 
contact them directly via WhatsApp.

---

## 🌍 Live Website

👉 https://salifumarong6-droid.github.io/Briks-FoodHub

---

## ✅ Features

- Browse food businesses by category
- Direct WhatsApp connection to vendors
- Gallery of Gambian food and businesses
- Contact form for new business listings
- AI-powered chatbot assistant
- Mobile-friendly design
- Covers all regions of The Gambia
